About DataTech

Who We Are

DataTech is a student organization at ShanghaiTech University dedicated to exploring the frontiers of data science and technology. We bring together passionate students who are interested in machine learning, artificial intelligence, data science, and other technical fields.

Our Mission

To create a discussion community for AI-related technologies at ShanghaiTech University, enabling students to learn and share knowledge. We support students in participating in data science competitions and AI research work, exploring the frontiers of artificial intelligence together.

Partnership

We are affiliated with Dadao College at ShanghaiTech University and maintain deep and close collaborations with GeekPie Club and ACM Club.
